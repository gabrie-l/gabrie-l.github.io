Welcome! I'm Gabriel, a PhD student for the Tri-Institutional Computational Biology & Medicine program at Cornell, Memorial Sloan Kettering and Rockefeller. Previously, I was a Computational Postgraduate Associate in [Dr. Hattie Chung's lab](https://www.hattiechunglab.bio/) at the Yale School of Medicine.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of California, Irvine, and a BSc in Computer Science from the University of São Paulo, Brazil. My research specializes in geometric and generative machine learning for spatial and single-cell biology.

In my free time, I enjoy coding in Rust, solving [Advent of Code](https://adventofcode.com), and configuring [NeoVim](https://github.com/gabrie-l/vim-config).
